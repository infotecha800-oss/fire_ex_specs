Operating Guide

The PASS Method

Four standard operating steps for all 8A Fire Safety Carbon Dioxide extinguishers. Remember: Pull, Aim, Squeeze, Sweep.

P

PULL the Pin

Break the tamper seal and pull the safety pin from the handle. This unlocks the operating lever.

A

AIM at Base

Stand 1–2.5 meters back. Point the horn at the base of the fire, not at the flames. Hold the insulated handle, never the horn itself.

S

SQUEEZE the Lever

Squeeze the handle lever firmly to discharge the gas. Release to stop the flow.

S

SWEEP Side to Side

Move the horn slowly side-to-side across the base until the fire is completely out.

Stage 01

Material Sourcing

Food-grade liquid CO2 sourced from certified suppliers. High-pressure seamless steel cylinder blanks procured from ISO-certified local Philippine mills.

Stage 02

Cylinder Fabrication & Testing

Cylinders are formed, seamless-drawn, and hydraulically tested at 19,000 kPa to confirm structural integrity for high-pressure liquid CO2 storage.

Stage 03

Liquid CO2 Filling

Precise mass of liquid CO2 charged into each cylinder by weight, not volume, generating a self-pressurized vapor space of 5,780 kPa at 21°C. 100% leak tested.

Stage 04

Quality Inspection & Labeling

Each unit receives comprehensive final inspection including precision weight verification, ISO label, production date, and serial number.

Stage 05

Distribution & Post-Sale Service

Units are carefully packaged and distributed. Post-sale recharge and hydrostatic recertification services available nationwide.
